Courses We Offer

We offer a variety of courses and programs designed to enhance your skills and knowledge in technology and engineering.

Robotics Course

Our Robotics Course is an interactive and hands-on program that aims to introduce students to the world of robotics and automation. We provide various robotics kits, including Lego-based robotic kits and different microprocessor-based kits, to help students learn the basics of robotics in a fun and engaging way.

Arduino Course

The Arduino Course is a hands-on program that teaches you how to use the Arduino microcontroller to create various electronic devices. Through this course, you will learn about the fundamental concepts of electronics and programming, including input/output, analog and digital signals, sensors, motors, and more.

Raspberry Pi

The Raspberry Pi Course is a hands-on curriculum that teaches you how to make different projects with the Raspberry Pi, including media centers, gaming consoles, web servers, and more. This course will introduce you to the essential ideas of computer hardware and software, such as operating systems, programming languages, and networking.
